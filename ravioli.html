Ravioli

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Ricotta cheese, shredded mozarella cheese, and shredded provolone cheese.
  • 1 tablespoon grated parmesan
  • 1 large egg
  • Dried parsley
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cold water
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Get flour and form a well in the middle of it. Form a well in it and add eggs, olive oil, and salt to the well. Next, beat it together with a fork.
  3. Mix evverything together
  4. Knead the dough for 8 to 10 minutes or until it is smooth. Wrap it and let it stand at room temperature for anywhere from 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  5. Prepare the filling of all the cheese and eggs.
  6. Divide it into 4 doughy balls and roll the dough through a pasta machine. If you do not have a pasta machine, then roll the dough will a rolling pin.
  7. Cut through the dough sheets and using either your fingertip or a brush, lightly brush the dough with the filling.
  8. Place the second dough sheet over the top and press around each portion.
  9. Using a sharp knife, cut between the mounds to form squares. Then, put it on a baking sheet.
  10. Prepare the sauce in a separate pan.
  11. Add ravioli to a large pot of salted water boiling over high heat for 4 to 6 minues.
  12. Drizzle marinara sauce on top.
  13. Enjoy!
