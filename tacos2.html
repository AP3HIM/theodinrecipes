Avocado Tacos

Description

Tacos are a staple in many households. They are quick, easy, and simple to make, and everyone loves tacos.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • Diced onions
  • Garlic salt
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro leaves
  • Jalapeno pepper sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven 325 F or 165 degrees C
  2. Combine avocados,onions, and garlic salt.
  3. Arrange corn tortillas in a single layer.
  4. Bake tortillas for 2 to 5 minutes
  5. Spread tortillas with avocado mixture
  6. Enjoy!
