Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish that features rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and dough. It is loved in America and all around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Dried oregano, black pepper, tomatoes, and basil leaves

Steps

  1. Make the dough. Pour warm water into a owl, sprinkle yeast on top, and let it sit for five minutes.Then, stir in cold water and salt.
  2. Knead dough until smooth.
  3. Form two dough balls and let them sit for at least 16 hours in the fridge.
  4. Make the pizza.
  5. Preheat the oven to 550 degrees F or 288 degrees C.
  6. Prepare the pizza.
  7. Put as much mozarella, oregano, and peppers on top as you want. Next, arrange some crushed tomatoes over the pizza, and then drizzle olive oil over top.
  8. Place the pizza in the oven and let it bake for 4 to 6 minutes
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven.
  10. Enjoy!
